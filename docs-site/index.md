## What Makes HiveMind OS Different?

Most AI tools are cloud services that hold your data hostage. HiveMind OS flips that model.

- 🏠 **Runs on your computer** — No cloud service. Install the app, it runs locally. Your conversations and data never leave your machine.
- 🏷️ **Your data, your rules** — Every piece of information is classified. You decide what stays private and what can reach cloud services.
- 🔀 **Works with any AI model** — Use paid APIs from OpenAI or Anthropic, free local models, or both at once. Switch anytime.
- 🧩 **Build exactly what you need** — Combine AI personas, automated workflows, and background bots to create the perfect assistant for your business.
