# Use Cases

HiveMind OS isn't just a chatbot — it's a platform for automating your business. Here are ready-made recipes you can set up in minutes, no coding required.

::: tip Everything happens in the app
Every use case below can be built entirely from the HiveMind OS desktop app — just point, click, and configure. If you prefer YAML, check out the [Examples & Recipes](/examples/custom-persona) section.
:::

## 📧 [Customer Support](/use-cases/customer-support)

Auto-reply to customer emails with AI that knows your product inside and out. Connect your inbox, upload your product manual, and let HiveMind OS handle the rest.

## 📋 [Daily Briefing](/use-cases/daily-briefing)

Start every morning with a summary of new emails, upcoming meetings, and pending tasks — delivered automatically before your first cup of coffee.

## ✍️ [Content Creation](/use-cases/content-creation)

Draft blog posts, social media updates, and marketing copy from your notes and ideas. Teach it your brand voice once, and it remembers.

## 📅 [Meeting Prep](/use-cases/meeting-prep)

Walk into every meeting prepared — with attendee context, agenda summaries, and talking points compiled automatically from your email and calendar.

---

## Not Sure Where to Start?

If you're brand new, try the **Daily Briefing** first — it's the simplest to set up and delivers value immediately. From there, add **Customer Support** once you've connected your email.

Need help getting HiveMind OS installed? Head over to the [Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart) guide first.
